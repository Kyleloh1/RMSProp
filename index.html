RMSProp Dynamics Visualizer

RMSProp iterated on the quadratic loss \(f(X)=\tfrac12 X^\top A X\); side-by-side 3-D phase portraits of position \(X\) and memory \(V\), in rescaled coordinates.

The loss matrix is built from a diagonal spectrum rotated by three Euler angles: \[ A \;=\; Q\,\Lambda\,Q^\top,\qquad \Lambda=\operatorname{diag}(\lambda_1,\lambda_2,\lambda_3),\qquad Q=R_z(\theta_z)\,R_y(\theta_y)\,R_x(\theta_x), \] with \(R_x,R_y,R_z\) the standard rotation matrices (angles in degrees below).

Rescaled coordinates. The plots and the initial-condition inputs use the rescaled variables \[ (X,V)\;\longmapsto\;\left(\tfrac{X}{h},\;\tfrac{\sqrt{V}}{h}\right), \] where \(h\) is the learning rate. The learning-rate field below is given in units of \(10^{-6}\) (so a value of \(1\) means \(h=1\times10^{-6}\)).
